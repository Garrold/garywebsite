KALLAX DISASTER

Kallax Disaster is an experimental electronic music project by the sound artistGary Fisher.
He is inspired by noise music, retro video games, and graffiti that he finds on bus stops.
The name Kallax disater is inspired by a photo found online of a set of Ikea Kallax shelves collapsed under the weight of too many records.
Gary has performed at festivals, in galleries, pubs, and people's living rooms,
he has a Masters in Sound Arts from University of The Arts London and an honorary PhD in Metaphysics.
